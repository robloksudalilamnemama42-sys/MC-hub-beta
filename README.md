⚒️ Minecraft Ultimate Hub & Utility Tool
Welcome to the Minecraft Ultimate Hub — an all-in-one web-based dashboard designed for dedicated Minecraft players. This project combines essential gameplay utilities, resource management, and community features in one sleek, dark-themed interface.

🚀 Key Features
Advanced Calculators:

Nether Coordinator: Instantly sync your Overworld and Nether portals with high precision.

Resource Tracker: (WIP) Calculate exactly how many materials you need for massive builds like Beacons or automated farms.

Enchantment Simulator: Preview potential enchantment outcomes for your gear.

Mod Download Center: A curated list of the most essential performance and gameplay mods (Sodium, JEI, Iris, etc.) with direct links to official sources.

Banner Design Gallery: A visual library of unique banner patterns to customize your base and shields.

Community Forum: A built-in messaging system for players to coordinate raids, share coordinates, and chat (supports local storage persistence).

Secure Authentication: Integrated with Google Account sign-in logic to ensure a personalized experience.

Multi-language Support: Fully localized in English and Ukrainian
